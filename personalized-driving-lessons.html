Professional Training for

“RTO & Documentation Services”

Gain the skills you need to safely operate large commercial vehicles with hands-on training and expert guidance from certified instructors.

Key Details
Duration:
30 hours (6 sessions of 5 hours each).
Location:
On-site at Kannan Driving School and on-the-road training.
Eligibility:
Valid driver’s license required.
Certification:
Course completion certificate for CDL test preparation.
Overview
The Heavy Vehicle Training Course at Kannan Driving School is designed for those who aspire to drive larger vehicles professionally, such as delivery trucks, buses, and other commercial vehicles.
With a focus on safety, control, and regulatory knowledge, this course ensures that students are fully prepared to handle the unique demands of commercial driving.
